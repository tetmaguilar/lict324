About Me

My journey in academia began during the 2021-2022 academic year, where I balanced dual roles as a part-time faculty member and an Administrative Assistant in the College of Computer Studies. Passionate about both teaching and technology, I was driven to expand my career. This led me to transition into a full-time probationary faculty role for the 2023-2024 academic year.

Before stepping into the world of academia, I earned my master’s degree in Information Technology at our university in November 2021, marking the start of my deep commitment to the evolving field of technology and education.
